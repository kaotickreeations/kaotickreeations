About Me

I'm passionate about using data to drive meaningful change. With a strong foundation in SQL, Python, R, and Tableau, I love transforming complex data into clear, actionable insights. My journey began in the healthcare sector, where I quickly realized the immense potential of data analytics to improve patient outcomes and streamline operations. Over the years, I've honed my skills in data manipulation, statistical analysis, machine learning, A/B testing, t-tests, z-tests, and data visualization, and I take pride in my ability to communicate technical concepts to non-technical stakeholders. Whether I'm writing SQL queries to extract valuable information, scripting in Python and R to perform data analysis, creating machine learning models to predict outcomes, or creating dynamic dashboards in Tableau to showcase trends, my goal is always to make data accessible and impactful. I'm constantly learning and staying updated with the latest tools and techniques to ensure that I can provide the best solutions to the challenges at hand.
